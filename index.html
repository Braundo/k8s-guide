K8s Guide




Your TLDR guide to Kubernetes documentation

`CONTENT CURRENT AS OF KUBERNETES VERSION: 1.28`


Welcome to k8s.guide, your concise companion through the world of Kubernetes. My aim was to provide a simplified, digestible, and easy-to-navigate version of the official Kubernetes documentation.


Explore topics at a glance, navigating linearly through the documentation (as laid out in the official documentation), or use the randomizer button to randomly jump through topics to refresh yourself on.


Note that I am not a frontend developer, so this is very much a work in progress. Feel free to add/edit/fix anything you see with a MR against the repo here.
